THE CHALLENGE

Navigating the Unseen World

Visually impaired individuals face daily risks and limitations that affect their independence and confidence in navigating familiar and unfamiliar environments.

Limited Mobility

Difficulty navigating independent paths in complex urban environments, relying heavily on others for basic transit.

Safety Hazards

High risk of collision with silent obstacles like hanging branches or e-scooters not detected by traditional canes.

Social Isolation

Reduced confidence to leave home often leads to significantly less social engagement and community participation.

TECHNOLOGY

How VisionAid Works

Our ecosystem transforms environmental data into intuitive feedback, creating a sixth sense for navigation.

Detect

LiDAR and ultrasonic sensors continuously scan the environment in 360 degrees.

Process

On-board AI analyzes obstacles, identifying object types and distance in real-time.

Alert

Haptic engines in the smart shirt provide intuitive directional vibration feedback.

Assist

Audio navigation via bone conduction guides the user safely around complex paths.
